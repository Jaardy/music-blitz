How do I add a pause / rests between notes in a Pattern?

You can pass a 'time' value to `triggerAttackRelease` which will delay the note, but it does not push subsequent notes along the timeline - meaing that they clash / crash.

Notes to play: A3, C4, E4, G4

Example 1

No delay, and no clash and no problems.

Example 2

With a delay on the 2nd note (C4), the pattern crashes

Note

After the crash, you may need to refresh the page to fix the audio

What I'm hoping to achieve

I want to inset a space / delay before the note 'C' and everthing else will move along accordingly 

  
   [1---][2---][3---][4---]
   [A---][----][C---][E---]

But what happens is...

The other notes are still on the same schedule, meaning the delay only affects one note and will clash / crash with the others 

  
   [1---][2---][3---][4---]
   [A---][----][C---]
   [----][----][E---][G---]
